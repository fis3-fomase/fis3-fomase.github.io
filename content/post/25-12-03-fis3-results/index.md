Project FoMaSE (Foundations for Macroprogramming-based Software Engineering) by principal investigator Roberto Casadei was one of the seven research projects that were selected for funding in FIS3 Starting Grant - Macro-area PE (Physical Sciences and Engineering), for a total budget of € 1.1M.
